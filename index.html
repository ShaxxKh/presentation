Regular Expressions

A sequence of characters that define a search pattern

How to create a regular expression

  1. Using two forward slashes like 
    /regular expression/
  2. Using constructor function 
    new RegExp(pattern)

Character classes

  • \ - switcher between special characters and literal character
  • . - ANY single symbol (that can be a number, letter, and so on)
  • \d - any single digit 0-9
  • \D - any symbol besides digits
  • \s - space
  • \S - any symbol besides spaces
  • \w - any letter or digit
  • \W - any symbol besides letters and digits
  • \b - borders of a word
  • \B - not a border

Meta character - " \ "

  • d - literal letter 'd'
  • \d - special character-class that mathces any single digit

Meta character - " . "

Regular expression
/king./g

The War of the 5 kings. The rains of castamere. I am the king. The Dothraki do things in their own time, for their own reasons. What is dead may never die. The War of the 5 kings. More pigeon pie, please.

Quantifiers

  • n{4} - searchs for 4 n-symbols in a row
  • n* - 0 or more of n
  • n+ - 1 or more of n
  • n? - 0 or 1 of n
  • [] - any of the symbol from this range [a,b,c], [a-c], [0-9]
